## Polling Lifecycle Table

| Interval | Starts | Stops | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| `topInterval` (1s) | `onMounted` (after first loadTop20) | `onBeforeUnmount` | Also stopped on `visibilitychange=hidden`, restarted on `=visible` |
| `hostInterval` (1s) | `openDrawer()` | `closeDrawer()` OR `onBeforeUnmount` | Also stopped on `visibilitychange=hidden`, restarted on `=visible` (if drawer still open) |

Both intervals: immediate fetch fires on visibility resume so UI catches up after any hidden period.
